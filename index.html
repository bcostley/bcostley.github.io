Mr. Costley's Class Library

Reading has been shown to be very beneficial to people for numerous reasons including increasing vocabulary, knowlwedge, and general well-being.

To support reading, I have a small library in my class for use during advisory and other times when a student might have an opportunity to read.

This library consists of my own books, most of which I have read. Students should treat the books with respect and return them in the same condition as they came.

To respect the parent/guardian right to determine what materials are appropriate for their students, parents may at any time deny access to any or all of the books in my library.
